# Introduction

[priority]: 10

You’ve found the Discord++ Documentation! These pages are dedicated to showing you all the ways that you can use Discord++ to make cool stuff. Whether you’re looking to create awesome bots for your community you're in the right place.

All of our [documentation is on GitHub](https://github.com/discordpp/discordpp.github.io) and we <3 corrections and improvements!

## Bugs

If you believe you're experiencing a bug with Discord++ open an issue on our [issue tracker](https://github.com/discordpp/discordpp/issues). If you want to report incorrect documentation, open an issue on our [documentation issue tracker](https://github.com/discordpp/discordpp.github.io/issues).

## Still need some help?

Join the [Official Discord++ server](https://discord.gg/VHAyrvspCx) for support and discussion regarding Discord++.

## Go Make Cool Stuff!

We love our developers, and we plan to keep making practical tools so that YOU can keep making cool stuff.

We can’t wait to see what you make.
